Background

I'm Sean Evans — an Asian American developer and engineering student born and raised in Arlington, Virginia. I'm dual-enrolled at Northern Virginia Community College (NOVA) while completing my Advanced High School Diploma at Arlington Career Center's Arlington Tech Program, with graduation expected in June 2026.

My coursework spans Web Design & Development, Object-Oriented Programming, Introduction to Discrete Structures, and Calculus — giving me both the theoretical foundation and hands-on skills to tackle real problems. In my Webdesign Leadership Capstone I served as a teaching assistant to NOVA instructor Christopher Jones, completing projects for him and fellow instructor Jeff Elkner — writing actual production code for real clients.

I've shipped an ESP32 hardware clock, built web tools used by NOVA instructors, and earned my Entry-Level Web Developer certification through Edube Interactive. My objective is straightforward: use technology to solve problems and keep getting better at it. I'm currently seeking internships and junior developer opportunities where I can contribute and grow.

Outside of code I'm a member of the Esports Club, a participant in George Mason's STEM Fusion program, and attended NOVA's Cybersecurity Camp in summer 2025. I also have basic conversational proficiency in ASL.
